I'm the Alibeez-to-Google-Calendar Connector

I will synchronize your leaves of absence from Alibeez to Google Calendar.

More precisely, when you create a leave request in Alibeez, I will add a corresponding event in your primary calendar within the next hour. I don't wait for the leave request to be validated. Furthermore, if you cancel a leave in Alibeez, I will remove the event your calendar.

For this to work, I need your permission to access your calendar.
